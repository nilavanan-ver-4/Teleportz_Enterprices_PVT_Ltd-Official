Data Protection

Privacy Policy

How we secure your international trade data and ensure global compliance.

Overview

At Teleportz Enterprises PVT Ltd., we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines how we handle data within our global import-export network, ensuring compliance with international regulations while facilitating seamless trade solutions.

Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies to manage your sessions and analytical tracking to understand how our trade platform is used. This helps us optimize our supply chain tools for your business needs.

International Transfers

As a global entity, your data may be transferred across borders to facilitate logistics, customs clearance, and international payments. We ensure all transfers meet high security standards.

Third-Party Partners

We share minimal necessary data with verified third-party logistics (3PL) providers, customs brokers, and financial institutions to fulfill your orders and maintain international trade compliance.

Data Retention

Trade documents and personal data are retained in accordance with international commercial laws and statutory audit requirements.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal information. You can also opt-out of non-essential communications at any time through our contact channels.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in global trade laws. Major updates will be highlighted on our homepage or sent via email to our active partners.

Legal Inquiries

For any clarifications regarding our data practices, please contact our data compliance officer.
